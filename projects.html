Our Impact

Real community care in action. See how we are making a difference.

August Holiday Feeding Programme

Objective: To alleviate food poverty and ensure child welfare during school holidays.

During the school break, many children in Majengo face food insecurity. We stepped in to provide nutritious meals to an average of 50 children daily.

This initiative was 100% funded through generous member contributions, showcasing local solidarity.

"This programme was a blessing for our kids during the break." - Rahma Saed, Parent

Mazingira Day Action

Objective: Ecosystem preservation and environmental education.

We are committed to environmental stewardship. In our recent drive, we distributed 200 trees and planted 50 indigenous saplings at SA Joytown School in Thika.
